Your QuickChat profile is your identity on the platform. Customize it with a profile picture, name, and bio to let other users know who you are.

## Profile components

Each QuickChat profile includes:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Profile picture" icon="camera">
    A photo or avatar representing you
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Full name" icon="user">
    Your display name visible to all users
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Bio" icon="message">
    A short description about yourself
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Email" icon="envelope">
    Your account email (set during registration)
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Setting up your profile

Your profile is created automatically when you sign up, but you can customize it anytime:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access profile settings">
    Navigate to the profile page from your account menu.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Update your information">
    Change your name, bio, or upload a new profile picture.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save changes">
    Click the "Save" button to update your profile across QuickChat.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Updating your profile picture

Add a personal touch with a profile photo:

### How to upload a photo

1. **Click the upload area** on the profile page (shows the avatar icon or your current photo)
2. **Select an image** from your device
3. **Preview your selection** - you'll see a preview before saving
4. **Click Save** to apply your new profile picture

### Supported formats

- PNG (.png)
- JPEG (.jpg, .jpeg)

<Tip>
Your profile picture appears in chat conversations, helping other users recognize you quickly!
</Tip>

### Profile picture features

- **Circular display**: Profile pictures are displayed as circles throughout the app
- **Multiple locations**: Your photo appears in the sidebar, chat headers, and message threads
- **Cloud storage**: Images are securely stored using Cloudinary
- **Instant updates**: Changes appear immediately across all conversations

## Changing your name

Your display name is how other users identify you:

1. Open the profile settings page
2. Edit the "Your name" field
3. Enter your preferred full name
4. Save to update

<Note>
Your name is visible to all QuickChat users in the sidebar and during conversations.
</Note>

## Profile visibility

Your profile information is visible to other users in several places:

### In the sidebar

- Your profile picture appears next to your name
- Users can see who you are at a glance

### In conversations

- Your photo appears with each message you send
- Your name is displayed in the chat header
- Your online status is indicated with a green dot

### During interactions

- Other users can view your profile details
- Your bio helps users understand your background

## Email management

Your email address is set during account creation:

- **Used for login**: Your email is your unique identifier
- **Cannot be changed**: Email addresses are permanent once registered
- **Unique requirement**: Each email can only be used for one account

<Note>
Make sure to use a valid email address during signup, as it cannot be changed later.
</Note>

## Profile updates in real-time

When you update your profile:

1. **Instant save**: Changes are saved immediately to the database
2. **Automatic redirect**: You're redirected to the home page after saving
3. **Live updates**: Your new information appears across all your conversations

## Required fields

All profile fields are required for a complete profile:

- **Full name**: Cannot be empty
- **Bio**: Must include at least some text
- **Profile picture**: Optional, but recommended

<Tip>
While profile pictures are optional, adding one makes conversations more personal and engaging!
</Tip>

## Profile creation flow

Your initial profile is created during signup:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Basic information">
    Provide your name, email, and password.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Write your bio">
    Add a bio describing yourself.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Profile created">
    Your profile is automatically generated with a default avatar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Customize later">
    Upload a profile picture and update details anytime from the profile page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Default profile elements

If you don't upload a profile picture:

- A default avatar icon is displayed
- You can add a custom photo anytime
- The default icon appears in all places your profile picture would show

## Privacy considerations

Your profile on QuickChat:

- **Visible to all users**: Any registered user can see your profile information
- **No private mode**: Profiles are public within the QuickChat community
- **Permanent data**: Information remains unless you update it

<Note>
Be mindful of the information you share in your bio and profile picture, as it's visible to all QuickChat users.
</Note>
