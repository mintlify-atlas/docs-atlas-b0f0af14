QuickChat's messaging system allows you to have private one-on-one conversations with any user on the platform.

## Starting a conversation

To begin chatting with someone:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a user">
    Click on any user from the sidebar to open a conversation with them.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Type your message">
    Enter your message in the text input field at the bottom of the chat area.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Send">
    Press Enter or click the send button to deliver your message instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sending text messages

Text messaging is simple and intuitive:

1. **Type your message** in the input field at the bottom of the chat
2. **Press Enter** on your keyboard or click the send button
3. **Your message appears** in the chat instantly

<Tip>
You can quickly send messages by pressing Enter - no need to click the send button!
</Tip>

## Sharing images

QuickChat allows you to share images in your conversations:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the gallery icon">
    Click the gallery icon next to the message input field.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select an image">
    Choose a PNG or JPEG image from your device.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Image sent automatically">
    The image is uploaded and sent to your conversation partner instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Supported image formats

- PNG (.png)
- JPEG (.jpg, .jpeg)

<Note>
Images are stored securely using Cloudinary and delivered with optimized performance.
</Note>

## Message status tracking

QuickChat helps you keep track of your conversation activity:

### Seen status

When someone opens a conversation with you:
- All your messages in that chat are automatically marked as "seen"
- This lets you know the recipient has viewed your messages

### Unread message badges

In the sidebar, you'll see:
- **Badge counters** showing how many unread messages you have from each user
- **Visual indicators** that update in real-time as messages arrive

## Message history

All your conversations are preserved:

- **Full chat history**: Access all past messages when you open a conversation
- **Chronological order**: Messages appear in the order they were sent
- **Timestamps**: Each message displays the time it was sent
- **Auto-scroll**: New messages automatically scroll into view

## Message layout

Messages are displayed with a clean, modern interface:

- **Your messages**: Appear on the right side with a purple background
- **Their messages**: Appear on the left side 
- **Profile pictures**: Small avatars appear next to each message
- **Timestamps**: Show when each message was sent

<Tip>
The chat area automatically scrolls to the newest message, so you're always viewing the latest part of the conversation.
</Tip>

## Conversation management

You can chat with multiple users:

- Switch between conversations by clicking different users in the sidebar
- Each conversation maintains its own separate message history
- Unread message counts update automatically as you read messages
