QuickChat provides a secure authentication system that allows users to create accounts and sign in to start chatting.

## How authentication works

QuickChat uses email-based authentication with encrypted password storage. When you create an account, your password is securely hashed using bcrypt before being stored in the database.

<Note>
All passwords must be at least 6 characters long for security purposes.
</Note>

## Creating an account

New users can sign up for QuickChat in just a few steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter your basic information">
    Provide your full name, email address, and password on the sign-up form.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add a bio">
    Write a short bio that tells other users about yourself. This will be visible on your profile.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete registration">
    Click "Create Account" to finalize your registration. Your account will be created instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Signing in

Returning users can access their account by:

1. Navigate to the login page
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Enter your password
4. Click "Login Now"

<Tip>
If you already have an account, you can quickly switch from the sign-up form to the login form by clicking "Login here" at the bottom of the page.
</Tip>

## Account security

QuickChat takes your security seriously:

- **Password encryption**: All passwords are hashed using bcrypt with a salt factor of 10
- **JWT tokens**: Secure authentication tokens are used to maintain your session
- **Email uniqueness**: Each email address can only be registered once

## Switching between signup and login

The authentication page makes it easy to switch between creating a new account and logging in:

- From the **signup form**: Click "Login here" if you already have an account
- From the **login form**: Click "Click here" under "Create an account" to register

## After logging in

Once authenticated, you'll be redirected to the main chat interface where you can:

- See all available users in the sidebar
- Start conversations instantly
- Update your profile settings
- View your online status

<Note>
Your authentication session is maintained securely using JWT tokens, so you won't need to log in again unless you explicitly log out or your session expires.
</Note>
